Frequently asked questions

Who can participate in lebraHacks?

Everyone is welcome to participate regardless of experience level, student status, or age. Each day there will be challenges geared towards beginners, so even if you’ve never coded before, there’s something here for you.

What if I can't code?

Everyone is welcome at lebraHacks regardless of their coding experience. Many participants use Local Hack Day as an opportunity to learn some coding skills and network with other beginners. In fact, we have programming and workshops specifically for hackers just starting out!

Who organizes LebraHacks hackathons?

Local Hack Day is organized by lebraHacks. lH is a hacker community.

How much does it costs?

It's free to participate in lH hackathons. Grab your laptop and join in on the fun activities and workshops.
